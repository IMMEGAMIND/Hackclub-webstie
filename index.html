There are many different types of beans

There are Green beans,

Kidney beans,

Bean sprouts and many more.

You can also cook beans in many different ways. Lots of people around the world enjoy these vegetables.

Here is one way to cook bean sprouts.

First you would need 1 pound of mung bean sprouts, 1 table spoon of neutal oil, 2 cloves of minced garlic, salt and black pepper.

1. This step is optional, but for a better texture, you should remove the hair on the bean sprout tip.

2. Wash the bean sprouts by rinsing them with cold water, then soak them for 5 minutes in water.

3. Then leave the sprouts in a colander for 30 minutes to dry it.

4. Heat up oil at medium-high in a wok and put the garlic in too. Continue to stir fry the garlic until it begins to smell nicer.

5. Put the bean sprouts in and stir fry them for around 30 seconds.

6. Add spring onion and continue stir frying for around 30 more seconds.

7. Your dish is now done.
